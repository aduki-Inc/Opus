Introduction

Hello, everyone! Let me introduce myself. My name is Farid Muhammad, and I’m currently join Dibimbing UI/UX Batch 15 Bootcamp, and I’m interested about User Experience (UX). This is my first portfolio for UX Research Case Study on Disney+ Hotstar app. I worked on this research together with Ghifara Diva and Nessie Gunawan. Through this portfolio, I want to share with you about my research journey for this app, and what I’ve learned during the process.

There will be about 3 (three) section that I will write in here. There are:

  1. Section 1 (Project Overview): Explain about research background, objective, methodology and tools that will be use, research timeline & process, and also target and criteria for the respondents.
  2. Section 2 (Findings): Explain about insights that already gathered based on the data (Qualitative or Quantitative data).
  3. Section 3 (Recommendations): Explain about actionable plan based on the objective of this research and the reasons.
  4. Section 4 (Lesson Learned): Explain about what I’ve learned during the UX Research process.
